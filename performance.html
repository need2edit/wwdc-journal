The Performance Pathway

Five sequential phases. Measurement first — never optimize without numbers. Then build time (developer feedback loop), launch time (user's first impression), runtime cost (frame rate, memory, network), and finally make performance a discipline rather than a one-time push.

All Items

Quick Wins

High-impact items that take less than a sprint. Most are config changes, instrument runs, or single-file edits that compound across the whole app.
